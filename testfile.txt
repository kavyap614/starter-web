 India has entered the third week of its 21-day nationwide lockdown imposed by the Centre to reduce the spread of the coronavirus in the country. With 354 fresh cases, the virus has infected 4421 people in the country. Countrywide death toll has climbed to 114. The number of recovered patients stands at 326. As per sources, several state governments have approached the Centre with request to extend the lockdown. The Centre is considering the request of state governments and advise of experts on extending the lockdown beyond April 14, sources said.
